    About Me

    I received my B.S. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the Ohio Statue University, Columbus, OH, in 2005. I was a NSF Graduate Research Fellow and received my Ph.D. degree in Biomedical Engineering from Duke University in 2013. I am currently a Whitaker International Program postdoctoral scholar in Biomedical Engineering at the Technical University od Denmark (DTU) in Lyngby, Denmark. My research interests include vector flow imaging, GPU computing, acoustic radiation force imaging, and diagnostic ultrasonic imaging.
